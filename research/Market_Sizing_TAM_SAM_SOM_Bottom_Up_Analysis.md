# Pangolin — TAM / SAM / SOM Market Sizing
## Bottom-Up Analysis | Digital Art Freelancer Escrow | May 2026

---

## Bottom-Up Methodology

```
1 digital art freelancer
× average annual commissions (24/year)
× average commission value ($150)
= $3,600 annual transaction value per freelancer
× total freelancer count (by geography)
= Gross Transaction Volume (GTV)
× platform fee (2.5%)
= Revenue opportunity
```
