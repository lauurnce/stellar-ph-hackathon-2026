QUESTION 3 — PROPOSED SOLUTION
================================================================================

Pangolin is a blockchain-powered escrow payment platform built on Stellar,
designed specifically for digital art commissions and creative freelance work.

Unlike existing platforms, Pangolin is NOT a marketplace — it does not help
freelancers and clients find each other. Discovery already happens on Twitter/X,
Instagram, Facebook, and Discord. Pangolin enters AFTER the agreement, acting as
the trusted financial layer that enforces what both parties already agreed to.

HOW IT WORKS (THE CORE FLOW):

1. Freelancer and client negotiate their commission privately (as they already do).
2. Both agree on price, scope, and a minimum guaranteed payment percentage
   (default: 20% — this is the floor the freelancer will always receive, no matter
   what happens, agreed upfront and enforced by smart contract code).
3. Client deposits USDC into a Pangolin Soroban smart contract on Stellar.
4. Freelancer receives on-chain proof that the money is locked and waiting.
   They can now start working with confidence.
5. Freelancer delivers the work.
6. Client reviews and approves — full payment releases instantly to freelancer.
7. If client disputes or goes silent: the minimum guaranteed % goes to the freelancer
   automatically. The remaining amount is handled via the dispute process.

THE KEY INNOVATION — MINIMUM GUARANTEED PAYMENT:

The single mechanic that no existing platform has: the client CANNOT get 100% back.
A pre-agreed floor percentage (10–50%, default 20%) always goes to the freelancer —
enforced by Soroban contract code, not by human moderation.

This solves the core asymmetry:
- Freelancers get paid for work they already did.
- Clients cannot weaponize the chargeback system after delivery.
- Both parties are protected from bad actors.

WHY STELLAR:
- Native USDC on Stellar — Circle-issued stablecoin, no bridging risk
- Soroban smart contracts — Stellar's native contract platform, purpose-built
- Sub-second finality — 3–5 second settlement vs PayPal's T+3 days
- Ultra-low fees — ~$0.00001 per transaction, near-zero platform overhead
- ~99% gross margin on platform fee

PRICING:
- Platform charges 2.5% on each transaction (paid by client, added on top)
- This is 75% cheaper than Vgen (9.4–10.4%) — with more protection
- Revenue from Day 1, no subscription required

LONG-TERM VISION:
- Pangolin Trust Score: on-chain reputation built from transaction history,
  tied to the user's Stellar wallet address — a verifiable freelancer credential
- EaaS (Escrow-as-a-Service): B2B API for platforms to embed Pangolin escrow
- Expand beyond digital art to all digital service freelancers in SEA

QUESTION 8 — EXPECTED STELLAR INTEGRATION
================================================================================

Pangolin is Stellar-native by design — not a multi-chain app ported to Stellar.
Every core product function runs on Stellar infrastructure:

1. SOROBAN SMART CONTRACTS (Primary Integration)
   - All escrow logic lives in Soroban contracts on Stellar
   - Minimum guarantee enforcement is a Soroban function — cannot be bypassed
   - Contract state is publicly verifiable on Stellar Explorer
   - Both parties can audit the terms before funding

2. USDC ON STELLAR (Payment Infrastructure)
   - Circle-issued USDC native to Stellar (not bridged from Ethereum)
   - Asset issuer: GA5ZSEJYB37JRC5AVCIA5MOP4RHTM335X2KGX3IHOJAPP5RE34K4KZVN
   - Stablecoin = dollar-pegged value, zero volatility risk for freelancer income
   - Payments settle in 3–5 seconds at ~$0.00001 per operation
   - Filipino freelancers can convert USDC → PHP via Coins.ph or PDAX

3. STELLAR ACCOUNTS AS USER IDENTITY
   - Freighter wallet (Stellar public key) = user account
   - No email, no password, no KYC at launch (permissionless MVP)
   - On-chain transaction history = the foundation for Pangolin Trust Score
     (future: reputation score derived from completed escrow contracts)

4. STELLAR NETWORK ADVANTAGES THAT DIRECTLY BENEFIT THE USE CASE
   - 3–5 second finality: freelancer receives confirmation faster than a GCash transfer
   - $0.00001 fees: platform can charge 2.5% and still be 75% cheaper than Vgen
   - 100 operations/second throughput: scalable from Day 1 to thousands of users
   - Built-in DEX: future feature — accept any Stellar asset, auto-convert to USDC
   - Stellar Testnet: full-featured test environment for safe development (Days 2–4)
   - Stellar Mainnet deployment: production-ready by Day 6

5. WHY STELLAR OVER ETHEREUM / SOLANA FOR THIS USE CASE
   - Ethereum gas fees (~$5–50/tx) make $50 commissions economically unviable
   - Solana has higher fees and more complexity for escrow state machines
   - Stellar's fee structure is the ONLY chain where 2.5% fee + escrow makes sense
     for micro-transactions common in Philippine art commissions (₱500–₱5,000)
   - USDC on Stellar = direct Circle partnership, enterprise-grade stablecoin,
     already integrated with Philippine on-ramps (Coins.ph)
