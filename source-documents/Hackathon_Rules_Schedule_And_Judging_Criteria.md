# ⭐️ Stellar Philippines Hackathon 2026 🚀

Welcome! You've officially joined a **100% free, 7-day online hackathon** designed for developers, founders, designers, and students who want to build real-world financial solutions on Stellar.

This hackathon is focused on solving practical financial problems in the **Philippines and Southeast Asia** through blockchain-powered innovation.

---

## 🌍 Program Highlights

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| 🗓️ Dates | May 18–24, 2026 |
| 🌐 Format | Fully Online |
| 👥 Team Size | Individual or Team (1–5 members) |
| 💰 Prize Pool | ₱50,000 |
| 🛠️ Platform | Stellar Blockchain |
| 🚀 Requirement | Mainnet-ready projects |
